Acai Bowl

Prep Time : 10 minutes Total Time : 10 min Servings : Serves: 1 Source : traderjoes.com

Ingredients

1 TJ's Organic Unsweetened Açaí Purée packet

1/2 cup TJ's Frozen Organic Mixed Berry Blend

1-2 tablespoons TJ's Unsweetened Vanilla Almond Milk

1 TJ's Banana, sliced and divided

1/3 cup TJ's Toasted Coconut Granola

2 TJ's Fancy Medjool Dates, chopped

1 scoop of your favorite protein powder (optional)

Directions

Add Açaí packet, berry blend, almond milk, and half of the banana to blender (and protein, if using). Blend until smooth (adjust almond milk if needed). Scrape smoothie mixture into bowl. Top with remaining banana slices, granola and dates. Grab a spoon and dig in.
